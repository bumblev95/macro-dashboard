Decision playbook

Green setup

HY OAS below 3.5%, unemployment below 4.7%, delinquencies stable, AI guidance strong. Stocks can ignore high bond yields.

Yellow setup

CCC spreads widen, BDCs/regional banks lag, claims rise, saving rate falls, or AI ROI doubts grow. This is the useful early-warning zone.

Red setup

HY OAS above 5%, unemployment above 5%, delinquencies accelerate, private-credit stress headlines appear, or AI guidance cuts. Bonds are no longer ignorable.
